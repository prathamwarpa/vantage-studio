THE WORK

We designed a comprehensive travel platform that showcases Meghalaya's diverse destinations with stunning imagery and compelling narratives. Each location experience is presented with practical details—difficulty levels, best seasons, and nearby activities—enabling travelers to plan their adventures confidently.

The platform now serves as both a booking hub and cultural ambassador, featuring seasonal experiences, community reviews, and immersive storytelling that captures the essence of Meghalaya's natural beauty and rich heritage.
