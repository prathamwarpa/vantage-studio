Himalayan Rides

Himalayan Rides is a bike rental and tour company based in the heart of the Himalayas. We offer a wide range of bikes for rent and guided tours through the stunning landscapes of the Himalayas. Our mission is to provide an unforgettable experience for every rider, from beginners to seasoned adventurers.

THE WORK

We developed a dynamic online platform that showcases the thrill of Himalayan adventures. The website features an intuitive booking system for bike rentals and tours, complete with detailed itineraries and photo galleries. The design captures the rugged beauty of the Himalayas, inspiring visitors to embark on their own journey.

From showcasing the fleet of bikes to providing essential travel information, the site is a comprehensive resource for anyone looking to explore the Himalayas on two wheels.
